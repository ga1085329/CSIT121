Shrimp Alfredo

The way my mother makes it

INGREDIENTS:

  • Fettuccine
  • Cooked Shrimp
  • Butter
  • Creme cheese
  • Heavy/whipping creme
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Boil water and add salt to cook the fettuccine, cook it according to the package
  2. While the fettucine boils add butter, chicken broth, heavy creme and garlic cloves to a pot. Then when the butter melts, add creme cheese so it can melt into the sauce.
  3. Then when the creme cheese has melted , let it bubble slightly then grate parmesan cheese into it and stir for about a minute.
  4. Once that has cooked for another minute add the shrimp to a seperate pan with butter and stir occasinally until they are fully cooked.
  5. Finally drain the fettuccine and add it to the sauce and serve with salt and pepper as needed.
